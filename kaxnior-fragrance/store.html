The Flagship

A room made of stone and quiet.

The first KAXNiOR space is conceived as a single grey room — raw mineral surfaces, diffused light, and four bottles set like specimens. A place to meet each scent beside the stone that seals it.

We took over a former engineering workshop in Seongsu and stripped it back to its concrete bones. Nothing was added that didn't need to be there. The walls are hand-troweled lime plaster, left imperfect on purpose — no two surfaces catch the afternoon light the same way twice.

A single board-formed concrete counter holds the four bottles, unlabelled until you ask. There is no music, no scent thrown into the air to announce itself. The room is kept at the edge of empty, so that whatever you're wearing — or considering — has nowhere to hide.

Visitors are met one at a time. A short conversation about what draws you in, then a few quiet minutes before the first stone is placed in your palm. We built the entire space around that pause.
