The House

A fragrance house, sealed in stone.

KAXNiOR is a sensory house where scent is treated as material — quarried, refined, and set. We build each fragrance the way one cuts a stone: slowly, around a single nature.

01

Shaped by nature

Every accord begins with a single raw material held at its peak — citrus pressed at ripeness, moss drawn from wet earth. Nature sets the direction; we only refine.

02

Sealed in stone

Each bottle is closed by a hand-selected raw stone. No two are identical — the stopper is the fingerprint of the object, a piece of mineral that will never repeat.

03

Worn as silence

Our palette is deliberately quiet: white, grey, the matte of unpolished mineral. The scent should be the only loud thing. Everything around it stays still.

The Signature

One stone, one scent. No two alike.

The raw-stone stopper is the heart of KAXNiOR. Each fragrance is crowned by a different mineral, chosen to echo what is inside the glass.

The Material

Quiet objects for a loud sense.

Glass is left clear. Labels are set in the smallest legible type. The only ornament is the stone itself — irregular, matte, unrepeatable. We design the object to disappear so the scent can appear.
